# Vatican City 2005

## Metadata

**Startdate:** 2005-01-01
**Enddate:** 2006-01-01

## Description

Following the death of Pope John Paul II in April 2005, the Vatican issued special coins during the period of Sede vacante depicting the emblem of the Apostolic Chamber.
